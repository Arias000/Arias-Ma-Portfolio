Background

Mantou Business School is a prominent EdTech platform specializing in digital transformation for enterprises. It offers premium online courses led by industry veterans in product, operations, and marketing. By integrating live-streamed masterclasses, online micro-courses, and offline workshops, the platform fosters a robust community-based learning environment.

Identifying key pain points such as narrow content coverage, limited user acquisition channels, high learning costs, and inefficient content retrieval, we initiated a comprehensive product redesign to address these challenges and enhance the overall user experience.

Problem

  • Limited course coverage and restricted user access channels
  • High learning costs
  • Low efficiency in content acquisition
