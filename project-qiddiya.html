Overview

The project is located in QIDDIYA EAST VILLAGE NORTH in Riyadh, KSA, and serves as a vital component of the "Resort Core," a district designed to position Qiddiya as a global destination. The Resort Core is envisioned as a high-energy, experience-intensive area described as a "theme park of theme parks," consisting of individual gated parks connected by a resort village.

This specific project encompasses six plots in the northern East Village, integrating diverse functions including the Plaza Hotel, Waterpark Overlook Hotel, RD&E Hotel (Retail, Dining, and Entertainment), serviced apartments, an escape room, and a food hall.

Challenge

The project faced significant extreme climatic challenges, particularly between May and September, when the site experiences intense solar radiation and high temperatures that make outdoor environments uncomfortable. Furthermore, the prevailing northerly winds carry high temperatures and intensity, failing to provide natural cooling.

Technically, the design was constrained by spatial and structural limitations, as the majority of the landscaped areas—including the ground level and roofs—are constructed over podium structures due to the presence of underground parking and functional spaces. Additionally, the East Village had to establish a unique brand identity while functioning as a cohesive link between the various gated theme parks within the Resort Core.

Solution

The design team proposed a dynamic multilevel landscape inspired by the lush greenery and tranquil ambiance of natural caves. To manage thermal comfort, a layered strategy was implemented, combining shading through building forms, mandatory 5-meter-deep colonnades, fixed and temporary shade structures, and the use of umbrella-shaped trees.

Active cooling solutions such as evaporative cooling (misting systems and water features) and optimized ventilation were also introduced. Each plot features a customized planting scheme, such as the "Sensory Garden" with cool color palettes for the Plaza Hotel and native evergreen gardens for the Escape Room. The hardscape and furniture utilize high-quality materials like reconstituted stone and colored concrete, using stripes and curves to mimic motion and support the project's wayfinding strategy.

Result

The final design successfully created a "Vibrant Oasis" that blends leisure and relaxation with a variety of activity experiences, including flexible social spaces for major international events like F1 racing. The advanced shading and cooling systems ensure a comfortable pedestrian environment, achieving the goal of providing 100% shade for at least 40% of pathways and external rest areas during peak summer hours (10 am to 4 pm).

Each plot established a distinct brand character, from the stylish and vibrant image of the Plaza Hotel to the family-oriented "Funhouse Hotel" concept of the RD&E plot, collectively enhancing Qiddiya's international recognition. Ultimately, the project achieved a seamless integration of landscape and architecture on podium structures, enriching the overall urban resort experience.
