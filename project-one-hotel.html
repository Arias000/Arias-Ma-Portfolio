Overview

The DG II 1-Hotel landscape design project is located in Saudi Arabia, situated on a rocky peninsula overlooking the spectacular panoramic views of Wadi Hanifa. The core design narrative is "Tranquillity Vista Exploration," with inspiration deeply rooted in the Kingdom's Oasis, Geology, and Topography.

Conceived as a "Mosaic Landscape" that surprises and unfolds, the design encourages guests to explore a series of distinct spaces tied together by a single unified aesthetic.

Challenge

A primary challenge in developing this ultra-luxury property was to take advantage of the spectacular location while effectively providing shade and privacy, which are key considerations for a luxury resort. Designers had to navigate the dramatic contoured landform of the rocky peninsula to create a functional yet contemporary space.

Furthermore, the design needed to maintain an authentic character that is "of its place," balancing modern luxury standards with the harsh realities of the local desert environment.

Solution

To address these challenges, the design features a lush, central "Garden of Life" Courtyard, an intimate sanctuary at the heart of the hotel planted with native species and fruiting trees like Guava and Pomegranate to provide natural shade and vibrancy.

This central core is linked to the perimeter via a series of "porosity portals" that lead to a Wadi-top walk, ensuring full permeability and promoting exploration. Additional features include a two-tiered organic pool inspired by the land's contours, the use of rugged natural stone walls, hewn stone steps, and low-level seductive lighting to create.

Result

The final result is a landscape that is truly rooted in place, built from the bedrock and working in tandem with the architecture to frame views of the spectacular wadi setting. The project offers a variety of sensory guest experiences, ranging from intimate pockets for contemplation to larger areas like the Sunrise Garden for social gatherings, weddings, and morning yoga.

By successfully blending contemporary luxury with the native Saudi environment, the design creates a unique sanctuary that celebrates the unique geology and beauty of the peninsula.
