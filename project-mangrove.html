Overview

Challenge

We were briefed to create the landscape design for MANGROVE ECO PARK in Western India, balancing ecological restoration with public access and interpretation.

Solution

We designed a coherent landscape system that protects and interprets the mangrove habitat while providing durable access, planting and wayfinding for visitors.

Result

A legible, accessible landscape that supports the eco park programme and strengthens the identity of MANGROVE ECO PARK in Western India.
