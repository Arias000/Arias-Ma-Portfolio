Overview

Qiddiya East Village North is an "Open Urban Resort" complex situated within the motorsport core area. By integrating quirky themed hotels (such as "The Funhouse"), serviced apartments, and maximized views overlooking the F1 race track, it offers visitors a high-energy social experience centered around racing events.

The project overcomes the extreme climate through innovative landscape strategies, including mandatory 5-meter deep colonnades and misting cooling systems. It serves as the cohesive element connecting the entire Resort Core and stands as an iconic leisure destination filled with artistic vibrancy and a sense of speed.

Challenge

The project site in Riyadh faces a harsh climate characterized by extreme heat and intense solar radiation from May to September, where even strong northerly winds fail to improve environmental comfort.

A major technical constraint is that the majority of landscaped areas across ground and lower levels are situated on podium structures due to the presence of underground parking, which complicates the integration of vegetation.

Furthermore, as a key part of the Resort Core, the project must balance a high-energy, high-intensity "theme park of theme parks" atmosphere with the essential need for visitor physical comfort in outdoor social spaces.

Solution

The design team implemented a multi-layered thermal comfort strategy, utilizing architectural forms, mandatory 5-meter deep colonnades, fixed or retractable shade structures, and umbrella-shaped trees to achieve at least 40% full shade coverage along pathways.

To overcome the structural limitations of the podium, slab-dropped techniques and raised planters were employed to successfully integrate greenery into the built environment.

Microclimates are further improved through active cooling systems, including mirror pools, misting systems, and water cascades that utilize evaporative cooling.

The landscape vision is defined by "Oasis" and "Dynamic" themes, creating diverse spaces like "The Funhouse" hotel to cater to specific target markets such as families and young adults.

Result

The project successfully established an "Open Urban Resort" that serves as the primary cohesive element connecting the various functional districts of the Resort Core.

Through these innovative landscape strategies, visitors can enjoy an artistic and comfortable outdoor social environment even under extreme climatic conditions.

The unique design of individual plots, combined with custom wayfinding paving, iconic water features, and distinct seasonal planting palettes, has secured strong brand recognition on the global stage while providing a high-quality experience for leisure and adventure seekers.
